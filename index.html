How to Play

Your mission is a multiple choice quiz. You will be given a question with 3 possible answers. Select the correct answer and move on to the next one.

You have a choice of difficulties. The easier the mission, the fewer the questions. We'll also provide you with more support.

Q Branch has developed a device to make things easier for you. Select the 50/50 to rule out one wrong answer. Don't count on this on harder missions though...

Once you've reached the end, submit your name to the leaderboards or try a harder mission. Remember, you can return home at any time by clicking on the page title. Have you got all that?
Now pay attention, Agent!

We are testing your knowledge of all things 007. Select the 'How to Play' button below for the rules of engagement. Press the 'Select Mission' button to choose the difficulty that best suits you. Finally, you can select the 'High Scores' button to see how you stack up with the rest of MI6.
